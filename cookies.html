🍪 Cookies Policy

Small data crumbs that help us serve you a better, greener experience — transparently and with respect for your privacy.

📅 Effective: 1st April 2026 | Revised: 15th April 2026

At Yugen Greenery Hub (“we,” “us,” or “our”), we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to nurture your browsing experience, understand how visitors interact with our mindful gardening platform, and deliver personalized content. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can manage your preferences. By continuing to use our website yugengreenery.in, you agree to our use of cookies as described below.

🌱 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences (like language or cart contents), and analyze site performance. Think of them as small seed packets that help our digital garden grow more efficiently.

🌿 Our Philosophy: We only use cookies that are necessary, functional, or help us improve your experience. No invasive tracking for the sake of surveillance. Your trust is our most valuable soil.

🍪 Types of Cookies We Use

We classify cookies into four main categories. Each serves a distinct purpose to enhance your journey through Yugen Greenery Hub:

Strictly Necessary 🛡️Essential for core functionality: secure login, shopping cart, checkout, and load balancing. Without these, the site may break.Session cookies (expire when browser closes), cart data, CSRF tokens.

🔧 How to Manage and Delete Cookies

You have full control. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect functionality (e.g., you might lose cart items or need to re-enter preferences). Below are quick ways to manage:

  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies via settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Visit “Privacy & Security” section.
  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can disable Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Cookie Consent Tool: We respect your choice. You can update your preferences anytime by clicking the “Cookie Preferences” floating button (if available on our site) or by clearing cookies via your device.

📋 Third-Party Cookies & Links

Occasionally, we embed content from third-party services (e.g., YouTube gardening tutorials, Instagram feed, or payment gateways). Those third parties may set their own cookies. We do not control those cookies, and we recommend reviewing their policies:

  • Payment Partners: Razorpay, Stripe — use necessary cookies for fraud detection and checkout.
  • Social Media Plugins: When you share a blog post via Facebook or Pinterest, those platforms may set cookies according to their own policies.
  • Google Services: We use Google Analytics with anonymized IP; data handling follows Google's Privacy Terms.

We do not sell or share cookie-generated personal data to third-party advertisers without explicit consent.

📌 Your Consent and Updates

When you first visit Yugen Greenery Hub, we display a cookie notice that allows you to accept non-essential cookies or continue with essential ones only. ⏳ Retention of Cookie Data

Most cookies we place are either session-based (deleted after closing the browser) or persistent with a limited lifespan (maximum 14 months for analytics). We do not retain cookie data longer than necessary for the described purposes.

🌻 Children & Cookies: Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect cookie data from minors without parental consent.

🛡️ More About Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a “Do Not Track” feature that signals websites not to track your activity. Our website respects DNT signals where technically feasible. For analytics, we have taken steps to anonymize and minimize data collection.
