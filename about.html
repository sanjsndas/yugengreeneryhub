Our Story: Rooted in Grace & Growth

Discover the heart behind Yugen Greenery Hub — where gardening meets mindfulness, sustainability, and community.

The Soul of Yugen

“Yūgen” is a profound Japanese aesthetic concept: an awareness of the universe that triggers emotional responses too deep and mysterious for words. At Yugen Greenery Hub, we believe that every garden is a canvas for Yūgen — subtle beauty, silent wisdom, and interconnected life. Founded in 2018 by a collective of horticulturists, ecologists, and wellness advocates, we began as a small nursery with a radical idea: gardening should heal both the planet and the human spirit.

Today, we are a thriving ecosystem of 10+ acres of organic cultivation, a knowledge hub for 12,000+ gardeners, and a beacon for regenerative living. Our methods are inspired by permaculture, biomimicry, and traditional farming wisdom from around the world. From rooftop farms in metros to backyard biodiversity corridors, we work with individuals, schools, and corporations to reimagine green spaces.

But more than a business, we are a movement. Every plant sold, every workshop taught, every compost bin built — we are planting seeds of consciousness.

🌻 From a Tiny Seed to a Flourishing Forest

In the beginning, our founders transformed a barren land in the outskirts of Bangalore into a living lab for organic gardening. With just 50 saplings and an unwavering belief in nature's resilience, we developed soil that was once arid into rich, carbon-rich loam using composting and green manure. Over the years, Yugen Greenery Hub evolved into a botanical sanctuary with over 700 species of native trees, medicinal herbs, and pollinator plants. We’ve organized over 150 community planting drives, restored 3 urban lake catchments, and donated 5000+ fruit trees to underprivileged farms. Our team includes botanists, permaculture designers, and wellness coaches — all joined by a passion for earth-friendly living.

Our philosophy extends beyond aesthetics: we practice zero waste across operations, use solar-powered irrigation, and all our nursery pots are biodegradable. Every purchase from Yugen contributes 1% revenue to rewilding projects in the Western Ghats.

📜 Our Charter for Earth & People

We are a certified social enterprise committed to United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs): Climate Action (13), Life on Land (15), and Responsible Consumption (12). Our organic nursery never uses synthetic pesticides or GMO seeds. We propagate heirloom varieties to preserve genetic diversity. We host free "Garden Therapy" sessions for senior citizens and people with disabilities, understanding that connection to soil lowers stress and improves well-being. Additionally, our internship program trains young agro-ecologists, empowering the next generation of green leaders. Since 2022, we've also launched the "Yugen Mobile Nursery" — a van that delivers plants and know-how to remote villages and schoolyards. Transparency, kindness, and ecological integrity run through every leaf we nurture.

Our impact indicators: zero-waste office by 2023, plastic-free packaging, 15 acres of land under organic management, and a thriving seed library with over 300 open-pollinated seed varieties. We invite you to visit our hub, sit under the banyan tree, and experience the quiet magic of growing life.
