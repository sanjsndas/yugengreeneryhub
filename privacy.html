Privacy Policy 🌿

Your trust is our soil. We are committed to protecting your personal information with the same care we give to every plant.

✅ Effective Date: 1st April 2026 | Last Updated: 15th April 2026

Welcome to Yugen Greenery Hub (“we,” “us,” “our”). We operate the website yugengreenery.in (the “Site”) and provide mindful gardening services, online consultation, nursery sales, workshops, and plant care resources. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, contact forms, social channels, and purchase our products. Please read this policy carefully. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express interest in our services, register for workshops, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us via forms or phone. The types of data may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full name, email address, phone number, mailing/shipping address (for plant deliveries).
  • Order & Billing Data: Payment method details (processed through secure third-party gateways), purchase history, product preferences.
  • Gardening Profile: Information you share about your garden space, plant interests, zone details, and consultation notes (only with your permission).
  • Communication Data: Records of your inquiries, support tickets, feedback, and messages sent via our contact forms, WhatsApp, or email.
  • Automated Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, time zone settings, and cookies (see our Cookies Policy).
🌱 Our Green Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Any data sharing is solely to fulfill orders, improve gardening experience, or meet legal obligations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Yugen Greenery Hub processes your data only for legitimate purposes directly related to our gardening community. Use cases include:

  • ✔ Processing and delivering plant orders, seeds, tools, and workshop registrations.
  • ✔ Responding to your inquiries, providing horticultural advice, and custom garden planning.
  • ✔ Sending order updates, seasonal gardening newsletters, exclusive discount offers (opt-out anytime).
  • ✔ Improving our website, troubleshooting technical issues, and preventing fraudulent activity.
  • ✔ Organizing competitions, feedback surveys, and loyalty programs to enhance user experience.
  • ✔ Complying with legal and regulatory requirements (e.g., tax records, consumer protection).

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & Indian IT Act)

For users within the European Economic Area (EEA) and under India's Information Technology (Reasonable Security and Procedures) Rules, we rely on the following legal bases: your consent (e.g., newsletter sign-up), performance of a contract (order fulfillment), legitimate interests (improving our services and preventing fraud), and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We respect the sanctity of your data. We only share information with trusted third parties when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Courier partners (e.g., Delhivery, India Post for plant shipments), payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe), email marketing platforms (Mailchimp), and IT hosting providers. These parties are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified services.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, we may disclose information to protect our rights, safety, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred with prior notice and subject to the same privacy commitments.

We do not rent, sell, or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising without explicit consent.

5. Data Security & Retention

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures — including SSL encryption, restricted access controls, regular malware scans, and secure server environments — to safeguard your personal data. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We retain your information only as long as necessary: order records for 7 years (tax purposes), contact inquiries for 3 years, and newsletter data until you unsubscribe. After that, we anonymize or delete your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, GDPR, or CCPA):

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime via the “unsubscribe” link or by contacting us.

To exercise your rights, email us at privacy@yugengreenery.in. We will respond within 30 days. No fee will be charged for reasonable requests.

🛡️ Children’s Privacy: Our services are intended for general audiences above 13 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

Our servers are primarily based in India. If you access our website from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or other countries where our service providers operate. By using our services, you consent to this transfer, provided that we ensure adequate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent protections.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., garden resource articles, social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before interacting.

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and remember preferences. Essential cookies are necessary for core functionality (e.g., cart, login). Analytics and functional cookies help us improve our content. You can manage your cookie preferences via browser settings or our Cookies Policy page.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The revised version will be indicated by an updated “Effective Date” and a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Yugen Greenery Hub after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
