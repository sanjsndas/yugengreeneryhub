About Yugen Greenery Hub

Yugen Greenery Hub is more than just a garden center — it's a philosophy rooted in the Japanese concept of "Yūgen" (profound grace and subtle beauty). We believe gardening is a meditative journey that reconnects humans with the earth. Founded in 2018, we have grown into a community of 5000+ passionate gardeners, offering organic seeds, heirloom plants, eco-friendly tools, and expert guidance.

Our mission: to cultivate green spaces, restore urban biodiversity, and inspire a self-sustaining lifestyle. Whether you have a balcony, backyard, or farm, we provide tailored solutions from vertical gardens to permaculture design. Over 300+ workshops conducted, 10,000 saplings planted, and countless smiles grown — join the movement.

Our core values: Sustainability, Education, Community, and Beauty. We partner with local nurseries, follow zero-waste packaging, and every purchase plants a tree in degraded lands. Let's make the world greener, one seed at a time.

Learn composting, terrace gardening, hydroponics and permaculture. Monthly workshops for beginners and advanced gardeners.

🏡 Landscape Design

Custom garden designs for homes, offices, and community spaces. We create Japanese gardens, edible landscapes, and pollinator havens.

🪴 Comprehensive Gardening Guide (Extended)

Gardening is an art and science. At Yugen Greenery Hub, we teach you everything from soil pH management, companion planting, seasonal crop rotation, natural pest control with neem and ladybugs, to water conservation using drip irrigation and rain barrels. We offer one-on-one consultations, garden audits, and personalized planting calendars. Our expert team has decades of combined experience in tropical, subtropical, and temperate gardening. We also provide maintenance packages: monthly pruning, mulching, fertilizing with homemade compost, and disease diagnosis via our plant health clinic. Join our online community to share progress and get real-time advice. Whether you dream of a lush cottage garden, a minimalist succulent arrangement, or a food forest, we turn your vision into reality.

Our special projects include therapeutic horticulture for mental wellness, school gardening programs, and corporate social responsibility (CSR) green initiatives. We have transformed over 50 institutional campuses into green oases. Additionally, we sell premium gardening tools: stainless steel trowels, ergonomic pruners, self-watering pots, grow bags, and cocopeat blocks. All products are ethically sourced.
