💡 Quick gardening Q&A

Do you ship plants nationwide?

Yes! We ship eco-friendly potted plants, seeds, and gardening tools across India. Live plants are carefully packed with breathable material. Shipping charges apply based on location.

Can I book a free consultation?

Absolutely. We offer 15-min free consultation for home garden setup. Simply fill the contact form or call our helpline. Paid in-depth landscape design sessions also available.

Do you host weekend gardening workshops?

Yes, every Saturday morning (9:30 AM) we host hands-on workshops: composting, terrace gardening, and natural pest control. Registration via website or walk-in.
